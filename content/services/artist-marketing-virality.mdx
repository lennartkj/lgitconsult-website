# Artist Marketing & Virality

Great work that nobody sees might as well not exist. We make sure yours gets seen.

LGIT builds audience and engineers virality for artists and musicians — from the first follower to the moment a room full of strangers knows your name. We come out of Leipzig's art and music scene, and we work the way attention actually moves now: a sharp idea, executed with craft, placed exactly where the right people are looking.

## What we do

- **Virality strategy** — we reverse-engineer what spreads in your corner of culture and build the plan around it.
- **Guerrilla & experiential** — unsanctioned, talked-about moments that *earn* coverage instead of buying it.
- **Content & rollout** — a release calendar and creative that turn a single drop into a campaign.
- **Production** — the music videos, visuals, and assets a campaign actually runs on.

## How we think

Reach is cheap and mostly meaningless; the *right* attention is the asset. We optimise for the people who move your career — fans, bookers, labels, press — not vanity numbers. And we won't promise virality, because nobody honest can. We engineer the conditions for it, then execute relentlessly.

We work with emerging artists, established acts, labels, and galleries. If you've got the work, we'll get it in front of the world.
