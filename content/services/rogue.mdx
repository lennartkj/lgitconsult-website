# Rogue — Guerrilla & Experiential

Most marketing is bought. The work people actually talk about is earned. Rogue is the line that earns it.

LGIT Rogue designs and runs art-grade guerrilla and experiential campaigns for brands — the unsanctioned moment, the installation, the stunt that gets photographed, shared, and asked about. We come out of Leipzig and we work the region first: Leipzig, Halle, Dresden, Jena, Erfurt. The aim is to own one thing — the rogue marketing firm of Mitteldeutschland.

## What we do

- **Concept & art direction** — the idea, designed to read as *art, not gimmick*. The craft is the difference between a brand people respect and a brand people scroll past.
- **Experiential & installation** — physical moments placed where the right audience already is, built to be seen, filmed, and spread.
- **Production & logistics** — the unglamorous part done right: builds, sites, timing, permits, removal. We direct; a vetted creative network executes.
- **Earned-media rollout** — the campaign doesn't end at the install. We engineer the share, the coverage, and the social follow-through so the moment compounds.

## How we think

Reach is cheap and mostly meaningless; the *right* attention is the asset. A single campaign that the region keeps talking about beats a media buy nobody remembers. So we optimise for signal — the people who matter to your brand — and for craft, because audacity without taste just embarrasses the client.

We run our own stunts in Leipzig, which means the portfolio is real and the method is proven on our own name before it touches yours.

## How we work with brands

Two ways in. **Project fees** for a single campaign — concept to coverage. **Retainers** for brands that want a running programme of moments through the year. First clients are regional brands across Mitteldeutschland; the format travels well to national luxury, fashion, and aspirational brands who want to be seen as braver than their category.

Tell us the brand, the city, and what you want people to feel. We'll come back with the idea.
