HTWK Leipzig needed their language learning platform replaced. The old one didn't work well on mobile, the auth was unreliable, and students avoided using it.

We're building the new version on Spring Boot with Kotlin. Frontend uses Tailwind. The platform has C-tests with real-time grading, flashcard trainers with spaced repetition, and course progress tracking. Auth runs through both SAML2 for university SSO and OAuth2 for external access.

Currently in active development.
