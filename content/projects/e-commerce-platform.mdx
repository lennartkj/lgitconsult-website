# E-Commerce Platform

We developed a comprehensive e-commerce platform for a retail client looking to expand their online presence. The solution includes product management, inventory tracking, payment processing with Stripe, and a customer portal for order management.

## The Challenge

The client needed a scalable solution that could handle thousands of products and high traffic during peak shopping seasons. Their existing platform was struggling with performance issues and lacked modern features that customers expect from online shopping experiences.

## Our Solution

We built a Next.js application with server-side rendering for SEO and performance, using a headless CMS for content management and Stripe for secure payment processing. The architecture includes:

- **Next.js Frontend**: Fast, SEO-friendly storefront with optimized images and lazy loading
- **Headless CMS**: Flexible content management for product information and marketing content
- **Stripe Integration**: Secure payment processing with support for multiple payment methods
- **Inventory System**: Real-time inventory tracking and management
- **Customer Portal**: Account management, order history, and saved payment methods

## The Results

The platform increased online sales by 45% in the first quarter after launch, with a 30% improvement in conversion rate compared to their previous solution. Additional benefits included:

- 65% faster page load times
- 40% reduction in cart abandonment
- 50% increase in mobile purchases
- Streamlined inventory management saving 15 hours per week

## Technologies Used

- Next.js
- React

- Tailwind CSS
