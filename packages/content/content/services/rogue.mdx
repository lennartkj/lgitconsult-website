# Rogue — Guerrilla & Experiential

Most marketing is bought. The work people actually talk about is earned. Rogue is the line that earns it.

Rogue designs and runs art-grade guerrilla and experiential campaigns for brands — the unsanctioned moment, the installation, the stunt that gets photographed, shared, and asked about. We are based in Leipzig and we work Europe: Berlin, Lisbon, Marseille, Milan, and the cities in between. The aim is simple — to make the moment people remember when the budget is long gone.

## What we do

- **Concept & art direction** — the idea, designed to read as *art, not gimmick*. The craft is the difference between a brand people respect and a brand people scroll past.
- **Experiential & installation** — physical moments placed where the right audience already is, built to be seen, filmed, and spread.
- **Production & logistics** — the unglamorous part done right: builds, sites, timing, permits, removal. We direct; a vetted European creative network executes.
- **Earned-media rollout** — the campaign doesn't end at the install. We engineer the share, the coverage, and the social follow-through so the moment compounds.

## How we think

Reach is cheap and mostly meaningless; the *right* attention is the asset. A single campaign a city keeps talking about beats a media buy nobody remembers. So we optimise for signal — the people who matter to your brand — and for craft, because audacity without taste just embarrasses the client.

We run our own stunts, which means the portfolio is real and the method is proven on our own name before it touches yours.

## How we work with brands

Two ways in. **Project fees** for a single campaign — concept to coverage. **Retainers** for brands that want a running programme of moments through the year. The format travels: regional challengers, national luxury, fashion, and aspirational brands who want to be seen as braver than their category.

Tell us the brand, the city, and what you want people to feel. We'll come back with the idea.
